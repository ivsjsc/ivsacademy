Simple 4-Step Process

1

Submit Application

Go in person and present your prepared documents to the receiving officer.

2

Biometric Collection

The officer will take your portrait photo and fingerprints.

3

Processing Period

Your application will be sent to the Public Security Department for Administration of Social Order for approval.

4

Receive Results

Notification will be sent via the VNeID app, SMS, or your registered email.
